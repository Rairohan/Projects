Albatross


Albatross is a Nepali Rock band from Kathmandu,Nepal.Formed in 1988 as a three-piece school band, Albatross has made a landmark in the NeplaiM Music scene.It all began when a couple of guys joined hands together with their rusted guitars and other old instruments to transcend the sound of Nepali Alternative Music, Each of which has significant influence in the music the band creates.


Albatross has the unique distinction of being the only band from Nepal to play at the South by Southwest(SXSW).Albatross is not only a legendary band from Nepal but has achieved worldwide success and notoriety.The band is largely influenced by Corrision of Conformity,Alice in Chains,Radiohed,Pantera,Incubus,Megadeth,Tool,Stone Temple Pilots,Soundgarden,Rage Against The Machine,Porcupine Tree.
